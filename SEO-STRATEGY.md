<h2>What is an AI phone receptionist?</h2>
<p>An AI phone receptionist is an automated system that answers business calls 24/7, takes messages, books appointments, and handles common queries — without a human on the line. Unlike voicemail, it holds a natural two-way conversation with callers using a realistic voice, instantly responds to questions, and routes urgent calls to the right person.</p>

<h2>How much does an AI receptionist cost in Ireland?</h2>
<p>AI receptionist services in Ireland typically cost between €149 and €350 per month, depending on call volume and features. CallMeIE plans start at €149/month and include 24/7 call answering, appointment booking, and sector-specific knowledge — far less than the cost of a part-time human receptionist at €1,200–€1,800/month.</p>

<h2>Is an AI receptionist GDPR compliant in Ireland?</h2>
<p>A properly configured AI receptionist can be fully GDPR compliant in Ireland. CallMeIE processes all call data within EU-based infrastructure, does not store recordings beyond legal requirements, and operates under a Data Processing Agreement. Businesses remain the data controller; CallMeIE acts as processor under Article 28 of the GDPR.</p>

<h2>Can an AI receptionist understand Irish accents?</h2>
<p>Yes. CallMeIE is specifically trained and tuned for Irish accents, including regional variations from Munster, Connacht, and Ulster. The system also responds in a natural Irish accent, which callers find more familiar and trustworthy than generic AI voices trained predominantly on American or British speech patterns.</p>

<h2>What happens when an AI receptionist can't answer a question?</h2>
<p>When a caller asks something outside its knowledge, a well-configured AI receptionist takes a message, captures the caller's name and number, and either transfers the call to a team member or sends the business owner an immediate SMS alert. No call goes unanswered and no enquiry falls through the cracks.</p>

<h2>Do I need an AI receptionist if I already have voicemail?</h2>
<p>Voicemail is passive — callers hang up 87% of the time rather than leave a message. An AI receptionist actively engages the caller, answers their questions, books their appointment, or takes a detailed message in real time. For Irish SMBs, switching from voicemail to AI answering typically recovers 3–5 lost enquiries per week.</p>
