§ 02 — Capabilities

What it handles for a salon

Six salon-specific jobs, tuned for Irish salon patterns — deposit-taking, stylist matching, cancellation list, and the honest "you'll need a consult first" moment.

I — Service & stylist

Right stylist, right service

Each service (cut, blow-dry, colour, highlights, balayage, treatment, wedding hair) has a slot length and a list of qualified stylists. The AI books the correct diary with the correct duration — no 30-minute slot for a 3-hour colour.

II — Deposits

Deposit for colour + extensions

For services over €120, the AI takes a deposit via a Stripe link sent by SMS during the call. Never takes card numbers over the phone. No-show rates at piloting salons dropped from 11% to 2%.

III — Cancellation list

Cancellation waitlist auto-fill

When a colour slot cancels (they do — it's the nature of a 3-hour appointment), the waitlist gets SMSed in priority order. First yes fills the slot. Stylist's day stays booked without a phone call to make.

IV — Consult first

"You'll need a consultation"

Some services need eyes on the hair before quoting — going from dark to blonde, major colour corrections, extensions. The AI knows when to book a free 15-minute consultation instead of a full appointment. Saves the stylist the "that's going to be four sessions, not one" conversation on arrival.

V — Missed calls

Missed-call text-back (60s)

Every call that rings out while everyone's got hands in hair gets an SMS within sixty seconds offering to book, answer a quick question, or arrange a callback. No prospective client goes to the salon down the street.

VI — Monday report

Weekly summary email

Plain email Monday: bookings taken, deposits collected, cancellations filled, questions asked, popular stylists and services. Ninety seconds to read, no dashboards, no spreadsheets.

§ 03 — Depth

Built for Irish salon realities

The specifics a generic "book an appointment" AI gets wrong every time — stylist hierarchies, honest colour pricing, deposit patterns that don't feel pushy, and the weekend cancellation flood that's the difference between a good week and a bad one.

01Colour

Colour quoting — honest, not scripted

The AI quotes a range, not a number — "€140–€180 depending on length and technique, stylist will confirm on the day." For anything dramatic (dark-to-blonde, colour correction), it books a free consult first rather than inventing a number.

Caller

02Deposits

Deposit-taking that doesn't feel pushy

Deposits apply only to services over €120 (colour, extensions, wedding hair, long-hair treatments). The AI frames it as "to hold the slot — deducted from your final bill" rather than "to commit you." Caller gets a secure Stripe link by SMS, pays on their own phone, slot confirms when payment lands.

03Waitlist

Cancellation waitlist done properly

When someone cancels a colour slot, the AI SMSes the waitlist in order of priority — regular clients first, then new bookings. First "yes" fills the slot. The stylist doesn't lose the earnings. The flexible client gets a last-minute slot they wouldn't have had otherwise.

§ 08 — FAQ

Salon questions we actually get

Written as they're asked — by salon owners in Limerick, Galway, Cork, Dublin. Answered honestly, including where this system is not the right fit.

01 Will it quote dramatic colour prices it shouldn't?

No. For colour transitions (dark-to-blonde, corrections, big changes), the AI doesn't quote — it books a free 15-minute consultation with your most experienced colourist. The quote happens in person, with eyes on the hair, because that's the only honest way to do it.

For standard services — trim, blow-dry, routine highlights — it quotes a range ("€120–€160 depending on length") and always says "stylist confirms exactly on the day."

02 Does it integrate with Phorest / Timely / Booker?

On Growth, yes — we integrate with Phorest, Timely, and Booker via their APIs. The AI reads real-time availability and writes bookings straight into your existing system. Client history is awareness-only on Growth; we don't modify existing client records.

On Starter and Professional, the AI runs off a Google Calendar diary that mirrors your salon system. Works well for single-location salons; less suited to multi-location groups.

03 How does the deposit flow work — is it safe?

The AI never takes card details over the phone. Ever. It sends a secure Stripe link by SMS during the call, the caller pays on their own phone (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Revolut, or card), and the booking confirms the moment the payment lands. If the deposit isn't paid within 15 minutes, the slot releases back to the diary.

Refunds for genuine cancellations (48hrs+ notice) are automated — one-click in your admin dashboard. No-shows forfeit the deposit, which is disclosed on the booking SMS.

04 Can the waitlist be too pushy?

The waitlist SMS is "Sorcha has 11am Saturday if you still want it — reply YES to grab it." No marketing, no repeated nudges. If the first person doesn't reply within 15 minutes, the second gets asked. Callers can opt out of the waitlist at any time (one-tap).

Salons running this feature report fewer no-shows and fewer empty slots on the calendar. Clients actually appreciate the option to grab a last-minute slot.

05 Can I keep my existing salon number?

Yes. We sit behind your existing number — set call forwarding on busy and no-answer (and after-hours) from your salon line to a CallMeIE number. The number your clients know stays unchanged.

Full porting is available on Growth: we move the number across via ComReg, takes about ten working days.

06 GDPR — what's stored and for how long?

Call audio: 30 days then deleted. Transcripts: 90 days then deleted. Bookings go into your salon system (Phorest / Timely / Booker / Google Calendar) — we don't duplicate client records. Deposit payments are handled by Stripe under their PCI-compliant processing; we never see card numbers.

A client can request erasure — email callmeie@proton.me and we purge within 72 hours. DPA on request for Growth; standard processor terms included with Starter / Professional.

07 Cancellation — am I locked in?

Starter and Professional are month-to-month. Growth has a three-month minimum because the Phorest / Timely / Booker integration is non-trivial to unwind. After that, Growth is also month-to-month.
