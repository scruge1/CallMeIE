CallMeIE is not a startup from a tech campus. It is one person — Adam Vaughan — who spent years on the floor of Irish service businesses and got tired of watching owners lose customers to a ringing phone nobody answered.

Why I built this.

I studied computing with software development in Tralee. Dropped out before finishing. Spent the next seven years working jobs in Irish businesses — five years storefitting and merchandising for Lidl contractors across the country, two years as a supervisor in Home Savers, a stint selling furniture.

In every one of those businesses, the same thing happened: the phone rang while someone was with a customer, on a delivery, doing stock. It went unanswered. The caller did not ring back. The owner never found out. The booking, the parts order, the new patient — gone to whoever picked up next.

When I came back to Limerick in 2026, I had enough grounding in AI and telephony to build the thing I'd watched Irish businesses need for years. An assistant that answers the phone when you can't. That knows your calendar, your prices, your most common questions. That handles Irish accents — because it was built for Ireland, not California.

CallMeIE is that assistant. I am still the only person who works on it. Every customer I have gets my direct contact. Every configuration I do myself. That will change as the business grows — but the standard will not.

"I did not build this for the tech market. I built it for the dental surgery in Limerick that misses calls on Monday mornings. The motor factor in Cork that loses trade orders at 5:01pm. The salon in Galway whose owner is mid-colour when the phone goes."

— Adam Vaughan, Founder

What CallMeIE actually is.

CallMeIE gives your Irish business a dedicated AI phone receptionist — configured for your calendar, your prices, your most common questions. It answers calls, books appointments, texts back missed callers, and escalates to you when something needs a human.

  • Live 24/7 — answers on the first ring, always
  • Handles Irish accents across all four provinces
  • Books directly into Google Calendar or your existing system
  • Texts a missed-caller immediately when you cannot answer
  • Escalates to your mobile for anything it cannot handle
  • GDPR compliant — data processed in the EU, deleted on schedule

Works for dental practices, hair salons, motor factors, solicitor offices, and most other Irish service businesses. Configured for your vertical specifically — not a generic call centre script.
