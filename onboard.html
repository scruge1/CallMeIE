§ 04 — Disclosure

Before you submit

GDPR. Your details and your callers' details are kept only for operating your service. Call audio is retained 30 days and then deleted; transcripts are retained 90 days for your reports. You or any caller can request erasure at any time by emailing callmeie@proton.me, and we purge within 72 hours. We never sell or share data with third parties.

Irish call-recording law. Under Irish law, callers must be informed that a call may be recorded or processed. Every CallMeIE instance opens with a short disclosure — we handle this for you, so your greeting stays compliant without feeling legal.
