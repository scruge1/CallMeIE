WonderWorld — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 April 2026

1. Who We Are

WonderWorld ("we", "our", "us") is an AI-powered content creation and scheduling platform. We are committed to protecting your personal data.

2. TikTok Integration

WonderWorld integrates with TikTok via the TikTok for Developers platform. When you connect your TikTok account, we request only the permissions necessary to post content on your behalf (video.upload, video.publish). We do not store your TikTok credentials.

3. Data We Collect

  • Account data: Name, email address used to register
  • Content data: Videos and images you create or schedule through WonderWorld
  • TikTok access tokens: Stored securely to enable posting on your behalf, revocable at any time
  • Usage data: Feature interactions, timestamps, error logs

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Authenticate you via TikTok Login Kit
  • Post content to TikTok on your behalf using the Content Posting API
  • Provide scheduling and analytics features
  • Improve the platform

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with TikTok (via their official APIs) and infrastructure providers under data processing agreements.

6. Data Retention

Account data is retained for the duration of your subscription. TikTok access tokens are deleted upon account disconnection or deletion.

7. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data at any time by emailing: privacy@wonderworld.app

8. Security

We use TLS encryption in transit and AES-256 encryption at rest for all stored credentials and content.

9. Contact

For privacy enquiries: privacy@wonderworld.app
